About

ChelaV is a comprehensive personal finance tracker designed to simplify money management. It enables you to record income and expenses, set and monitor daily, weekly, and monthly budgets, and efficiently manage EMIs, loans, and credit card bills—all in one place. With clear insights and organized tracking, ChelaV helps you stay in control of your finances and achieve your financial goals with confidence.
